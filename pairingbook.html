VEGGIE RECIPE BOOK

BOOK LAYOUT / PHOTOGRAPHY / ILLUSTRATION

The original concept of the 'Veggie Recipe Book' was thought of by myself.

Indulge in a culinary adventure with this delightful recipe book, which explores the fun pairing of vegetables and eggs; playfully and creatively. From healthy breakfast options to tantalizing lunch and dinner dishes, this book promises to ignite your taste buds and inspire your inner chef.

I designed the book using Adobe InDesign; created the illustrations in Adobe Illustrator; shot the photos myself; and edited them with Adobe Photoshop.

The type and mockup were sourced online.
