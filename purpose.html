PURPOSE CONTRACTING

BRANDING / LOGO DESIGN / PACKAGING / HTML

The original concept of ‘Purpose Contracting’ was thought of by myself.

‘Purpose Contracting’ prides itself on its hardworking employees and energetic spirit, and I created promotional materials that would benefit and showcase its identity.

I designed the logo and packaging using Adobe Illustrator while crafting the promotional materials with Adobe InDesign. The website is unpublished but fully functional and was created using a template customized with HTML coding in Adobe Dreamweaver.

The photographs and mockups were sourced online.
