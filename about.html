ALLI THOMPSON

GRAPHIC DESIGNER AND ILLUSTRATOR

Alli Thompson is a graphic design student entering her third–year at Conestoga College. Her work involves creating bold and fun designs that enhance and build identities. Alli’s strong interest in branding and logo design was recognized in 2022 when she was awarded the RGD First–Year Artista Award for logo design and brand identity. She continues to learn new skills and keep up with the latest design trends.
