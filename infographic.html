BEAUTIES OF THE NIGHT

INFOGRAPHIC / ILLUSTRATION

The original concept of 'Beauties of the Night' was thought of by myself.

This illustrative infographic, 'Beauties of the Night,' offers a fascinating glimpse into the captivating world of lightning bugs. From their unique bioluminescent abilities to their intricate mating rituals, this piece of art is a tribute to the wonders of nature.

I designed the infographic in Adobe InDesign and created the illustrations using Procreate.

The mockup was sourced online.
